Overview

Nansubuga Barbra Musoke was admitted to the bar and has been in private practice for over five (5) years. She is an advocate of the High court and all courts surbodinate thereto. She holds a bachelors degree in law (with honours) from Makerere University and a Diploma in legal practice from Law Development Center.

Her area of specialty is land transactions covering all aspects of land conveyancing such as leases, mortgages and transfers among others, Intellectual properties law, Succession Law, laws governing Banking Institutions with a wealth of experience acquired while working as a Legal Head at Pearl Microfinance Limited.

Civil Litigation, Incorporation of Companies and Non Government Organizations and Mediation Procedures; She has also worked with one of the prominent Law firms M/s MUBIRU-MUSOKE, MUSISI & CO ADVOCATES in Kampala as a Legal Associate where she acquired most of her Legal profession Experience.
