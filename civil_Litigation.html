Civil Litigation

Bearing in mind the protractions and sundry vagaries of the ilk pertaining to litigation, it is our firmâ€™s policy to employ litigation only as the last resort.

We advise our clientele on the quickest and most cost effective approach to resolution of a dispute.
We as much as possible try to resolve disputes out of court. Mindful of the inadvertent delays and frustrations within the judicial system in determination of disputes especially in commercial matters where a judgment obtained five (5) years after filing of a suit could be rendered insignificant owing to the increasing inflation and devaluation of the shilling.

However, when a mutual resolution between parties is untenable, we refer the dispute to court for adjudication and we effectively represent our clientele in that regard. We have vast experience in both civil and criminal litigation.
